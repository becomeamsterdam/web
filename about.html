The story

What is BECOME?

BECOME is a premium personal training and community concept built in Amsterdam Oud Zuid — one of the city's most respected neighbourhoods. Founded by personal trainer Bas de Jong, BECOME was built on a simple conviction: that training done properly changes more than your body. It changes how you think, how you carry yourself and who you spend time with.

We specialise in strength training, performance coaching and community-driven events for ambitious professionals. People who take what they do seriously — and want to bring the same seriousness to how they train.

The philosophy

Three pillars.
One concept.

TRAIN

Physical growth

Precision personal training and strength coaching. Technically excellent, individually programmed, and always progressing. We build real strength — not just the appearance of it.

CONNECT

Social growth

Sport clinics, network events and community sessions that bring ambitious people together through shared physical effort. Real friendships built in real environments.

BECOME

Mental growth

Training consistently at a high level builds mental clarity, discipline and confidence that extends well beyond the gym. BECOME is an investment in who you are becoming.
