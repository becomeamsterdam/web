Duo Training.

Train with a partner. Keep the standard high.

What to expect

Shared effort, personal attention.

Duo training is for two people who want to train together without losing coaching quality. You bring a partner, friend or colleague. We bring structure, programming and technical direction.

It is a strong option when accountability matters and training together makes the process more enjoyable.
